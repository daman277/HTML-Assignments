INTRODUCTION ABOUT WEB DESIGN

Web design is like decorating a digital house. It's all about making websites look pretty and easy to use. Web designers choose colors, fonts, and pictures to create a great online experience for you. They're the ones who make sure everything on a website is in the right place and easy to click. So, when you visit a website and find it attractive and easy to navigate, that's the magic of web design at work!
