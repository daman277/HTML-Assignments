INTRODUCTION ABOUT WEB DEVELOPMENT

Web development is like building and designing stuff on the internet. It's how websites and online tools are created. Think of it as a mix of creativity and computer skills. Web developers make things look good on the screen (the part you see) and work behind the scenes (the part you don't see) to make sure everything runs smoothly. It's an exciting field where people make cool things happen online!
